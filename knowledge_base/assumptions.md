# Assumptions

This document captures the foundational assumptions used in the Readiness Intelligence Platform.

## Core Assumptions

- Recent engagement is more predictive than historical engagement.
- Readiness is independent from eligibility.
- Passive email sends should not increase readiness.
- Account intent is supportive but not dominant.
- Deterministic scoring is preferred over generative scoring.

---

## Additional Assumptions

- High-quality event interactions carry more signal than low-touch campaigns.
- Missing or ambiguous profile data increases outreach risk.
- Blocked records should remain visible for analytics, even when outreach is prohibited.
- A single strong positive signal can raise readiness, but quality and eligibility must still be validated.
- Data quality penalties are necessary to avoid inflating scores from automated or noisy behaviors.
