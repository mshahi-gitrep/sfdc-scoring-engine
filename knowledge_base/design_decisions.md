# Design Decision 001

Decision:
Use persona-driven synthetic generation rather than purely random field generation.

Reason:
CRM behavior is driven by people, not fields.

A CISO, Security Analyst, Competitor Employee, and Partner Contact exhibit different:

* conversion rates
* engagement patterns
* lead sources
* account fit
* campaign responce
* contact formation patterns


This creates realistic downstream scoring behavior.

Impact:
Improves realism of readiness scoring and stress-tests model fairness.
